Autotune your Insulin Pump

This project was started at the Nightscout Foundation NFHack2017.1 Hackathon to provide people who are NOT running OpenAPS - or even Nightscout - with the opportunity to tune pump setttings based on recommendations from the AutoTune oref0 algorithm.
